# **Canonical Sayings Index**

*A thematic shelf of Gospel passages for a Living Way reading*

> This index stays inside the canonical Gospels. It groups sayings and scenes by theme so readers can move quickly between the strongest passages for an inward, contemplative reading of Jesus.

---

## **Kingdom Near and Hidden**

* `Luke 17:20-21` — the Kingdom not coming by outward observation
* `Matthew 13:31-32` — mustard seed
* `Matthew 13:33` — leaven
* `Matthew 13:44` — treasure hidden in a field
* `Matthew 13:45-46` — pearl of great price
* `Mark 4:26-29` — seed growing secretly

## **Childlikeness and Receptivity**

* `Matthew 18:1-4` — become like children
* `Mark 10:13-16` — receive the Kingdom like a child

## **Inner Purity over Outer Religion**

* `Mark 7:14-23` — what defiles comes from within
* `Matthew 23:25-28` — clean the inside of the cup
* `Luke 11:39-41` — inside full of greed and wickedness

## **Light, Vision, and Singleness**

* `Matthew 6:22-23` — the eye as lamp of the body
* `Luke 11:34-36` — whole body full of light
* `John 1:4-9` — life as the light of human beings
* `John 8:12` — the light of life

## **Spirit, Living Water, and Inward Worship**

* `John 4:10-14` — living water
* `John 4:21-24` — worship in spirit and truth
* `John 7:37-39` — rivers of living water from within

## **Truth, Abiding, and Freedom**

* `John 8:31-32` — truth will set you free
* `John 3:20-21` — doing truth and coming to the light
* `John 14:6` — the way, the truth, and the life
* `John 15:1-10` — abide in the vine

## **Death, Life, and the Passing Now**

* `John 5:24` — passed from death to life
* `John 8:51` — not seeing death
* `John 12:24-25` — grain of wheat; losing life to keep it
* `Matthew 16:24-26` — lose life to find it

## **Oneness and Participation in Divine Life**

* `John 17:20-23` — that they may all be one
* `John 10:34` — "you are gods"

## **The Divided Heart and the Forces That Bind**

* `Matthew 5:21-30` — anger and lust begin inwardly
* `Matthew 6:24` — cannot serve two masters
* `Mark 4:11-12` — seeing without seeing
* `John 12:31` — ruler of this world

## **Stories That Fit the Living Way Reading**

* `John 4` — the Samaritan woman at the well
* `Luke 10:38-42` — Mary and Martha
* `Mark 10:17-27` — the rich young ruler
* `Matthew 23:25-26` — the inside of the cup
* `Mark 8:22-26` — gradual healing of the blind man
* `John 9` — the man born blind
* `Mark 5:25-34` — the woman with the flow of blood
* `Mark 4:1-20` — the parable of the sower

---

## **Themes That Do Not Transfer Cleanly from Thomas and Mary**

These themes can be echoed canonically, but not usually in the same exact form:

* `Thomas 3` — "know yourselves"
* `Thomas 22` — "make the two one"
* `Mary` — the ascent past the Powers in a developed sequence
* explicit anti-Paul or anti-atonement teaching stated by Jesus in the canonical Gospels

For a more careful canon-only treatment, see [The_Canonical_Way_of_Jesus.md](The_Canonical_Way_of_Jesus.md).
