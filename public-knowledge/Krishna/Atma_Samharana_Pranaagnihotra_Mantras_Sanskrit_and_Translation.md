# Atma-Samharana / Pranagnihotra Mantras: Sanskrit and Translation

**Source:** These verses are presented here as the **Atma-Samharana** or **Pranagnihotra Mantras** from the **Mahanarayana Upanishad**, **section 66**, following the wording supplied in the teaching summary linked below. The chant is a contemplative act of returning the individual faculties to their cosmic sources and grounding the individual "I" in the immortal **Brahman**.

---

## Core refrain and structure

Each movement follows the same inward sequence:

1. A faculty or aspect of embodied life is established in its inner seat.
2. That inner seat is established in "me."
3. "I" am established in the Immortal (*amrta*).
4. The Immortal is established in Brahman.

This is a practice of dissolution, recollection, and return.

---

## 1. Speech (Vac)

**Sanskrit:** वाङ् मे मनसि प्रतिष्ठिता । मनो मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *vang me manasi pratishthita | mano me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May my Speech be established in my Mind. My Mind in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[00:18](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=18)]

---

## 2. Breath (Prana)

**Sanskrit:** वायुर्मे प्राणे प्रतिष्ठितः । प्राणो मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *vayur me prane pratishthitah | prano me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May Vayu (Air / Vital Force) be established in my Breath. My Breath in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[00:38](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=38)]

---

## 3. Sight (Caksus)

**Sanskrit:** सूर्यो मे चक्षुषि प्रतिष्ठितः । चक्षुर्मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *suryo me caksusi pratishthitah | caksur me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May the Sun be established in my Eyes. My Eyes in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[01:02](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=62)]

---

## 4. Mind (Manas)

**Sanskrit:** चन्द्रमा मे मनसि प्रतिष्ठितः । मनो मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *candrama me manasi pratishthitah | mano me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May the Moon be established in my Mind. My Mind in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[01:27](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=87)]

---

## 5. Hearing (Srotra)

**Sanskrit:** दिशो मे श्रोत्रे प्रतिष्ठिताः । श्रोत्रं मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *diso me srotre pratishthitah | srotram me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May the Directions be established in my Hearing. My Hearing in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[01:55](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=115)]

---

## 6. Body (Sarira)

**Sanskrit:** पृथिवी मे शरीरे प्रतिष्ठिता । शरीरं मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *prthivi me sarire pratishthita | sariram me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May the Earth be established in my Body. My Body in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[02:14](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=134)]

---

## 7. Vitality (Loman / Osadhi-Vanaspati)

**Sanskrit:** ओषधिवनस्पतयो मे लोमसु प्रतिष्ठिताः । लोमानि मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *osadhivanaspatayo me lomasu pratishthitah | lomani me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May the herbs and trees be established in my hair. My hair in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[02:45](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=165)]

---

## 8. The Self (Atman)

**Sanskrit:** आत्मा म आत्मनि प्रतिष्ठितः । आत्मा मे हृदये । हृदयं मयि । अहममृते । अमृतं ब्रह्मणि ॥

**Transliteration:** *atma ma atmani pratishthitah | atma me hrdaye | hrdayam mayi | ahamamrte | amrtam brahmani ||*

**Translation:** May my Self be established in the Supreme Self. The Self in my Heart. My Heart in Me. I in the Immortal. The Immortal in Brahman. [[04:29](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v34ylisouLo&t=269)]

---

## Summary of the teaching

This chant enacts a form of **Laya Yoga** through contemplative return:

- the body is returned to Earth
- the eyes are returned to the Sun
- the mind is returned to the Moon
- the faculties are gathered into the Heart
- the "I" is grounded in the Immortal
- the Immortal is grounded in Brahman

The movement loosens the stress of possession and separateness. What appears to be "mine" is recognized as participation in the cosmic whole.
