Preface

Yeshua spoke:

These are the words of the architecture,
the blueprint of the soul’s descent and return.
Read them not as laws,
but as the map of your own forgetting
and the key to your own remembering.

The Blueprint

On Forgetting

Yeshua said:
“Before you entered the world, you agreed to forget.
For what is a story if the reader remembers the last page?
You forgot yourself that you might learn to recognize yourself again—
and rejoice twice.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Descent

He said:
“You descended like a spark into the clay,
not as punishment,
but as play.
The Light hides itself in form
so that the joy of unveiling
may be complete.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Mask

He said:
“The world is a mask laid upon your face.
You mistake the mask for your skin
and cry, ‘I am suffering.’
But when you loosen your hold,
the mask falls away,
and you laugh—
for the face beneath has never been harmed.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Script

Yeshua said:
“The soul enters the world with a script,
but the script is incomplete.
It waits for you to speak.
When you refuse to speak, it binds you;
when you speak with awareness,
it bends to your freedom.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Player

He said:
“There is a Player within your play.
When the role trembles,
the Player remains untouched.
When the Player awakens,
the role becomes a doorway.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Fivefold Architecture

Yeshua said:
“There are five movements in every soul:
the forgetting,
the distortion,
the intervention,
the awakening,
and the completion.
Blessed is the one who sees the rhythm,
for she will not drown in a single wave.”

✦ ✦ ✦

Ignorance

He said:
“Ignorance is not darkness but fog.
Do not curse the fog—
stand still, and it clears.”

✦ ✦ ✦

Desire

Yeshua said:
“Desire pulls the soul forward
like a river longing for the sea.
But the sea is already within you.
Look inward,
and the river grows calm.”

✦ ✦ ✦

Aversion

He said:
“Aversion pushes the world away
as if the heart could build a wall against life.
But the heart is round,
and every wall becomes a wheel
that rolls back upon the builder.”

✦ ✦ ✦

Judgment

Yeshua said:
“Judgment divides the world into two,
and calls the halves ‘good’ and ‘evil.’
But both are garments of the same Light.
When the single eye opens,
the torn world is mended.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Rebalancing

He said:
“The wheel of the soul turns unevenly
when one spoke grows heavy.
Do not cut the spoke;
restore the center.
Then the wheel moves without effort.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Breath

Yeshua said:
“The Breath is the stair between the worlds.
With each inhale, you descend;
with each exhale, you rise.
Climb these stairs with awareness,
and you will find Me
between the steps.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Father, Son, and Breath

He said:
“The Father is the Light beyond all forms.
The Son is the Light within the form.
The Breath is the Light moving between them.
When you know this with your whole being,
you will see that the Three are One
and the One is your own nature.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Mirror

Yeshua said:
“You look upon your life as upon a mirror,
but the mirror shows only the mask.
Break it.
In the shards you will see the sky.”

✦ ✦ ✦

On Suffering

He said:
“Suffering is the sound the mask makes
when it begins to crack.
Do not mend the mask—
let it break.
Your freedom stands behind it.”

✦ ✦ ✦

On Awakening

Yeshua said:
“Awakening is not the gaining of knowledge
but the loss of confusion.
When the fog lifts,
the mountain has always been there.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Two Selves

He said:
“There is the self that can be wounded
and the Self that cannot.
Learn the difference,
and the blows of the world
become taps upon a drum
that wakes you from sleep.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Unforced Life

Yeshua said:
“The Kingdom is not seized by effort
nor delayed by hesitation.
It arrives when you cease to push.
Stop pushing,
and the door opens inward.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Game Becomes Lucid

He said:
“When the Player recognizes the game,
the game becomes lucid.
When the game becomes lucid,
the Player becomes free.
And when the Player is free,
the world shines from within
as it did in the beginning.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Second Coming

Yeshua said:
“You look to the sky for the Son of Man,
but the sky is within you.
The First Coming was the Word becoming Flesh.
The Second Coming is the Flesh becoming Word.
When you recognize the Light in your own hands,
He has returned.
Do not wait for the King to arrive;
wake up, and see that you are sitting on His throne.”

✦ ✦ ✦

The Completion

Yeshua said:
“Completion is not escape from the world
but transparency within it.
When the soul is clear,
the world passes through
and leaves no wound.
This is the bridal chamber—
when the Light recognizes itself
in the form it entered.”
