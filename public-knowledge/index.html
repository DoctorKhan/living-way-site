Library by Teacher

This library is easiest to use if you read it in layers—or if you pick a path by teacher (including a practice-first, no-creed order for skeptical readers). Meditation and contemplation can change perception; you are not asked to sign onto beliefs before you look. Only the Wisdom Gospels are presented here as closest to the original Yeshua stream; the rest are modern works inspired by or derived from these traditions.

Yeshua

The Living Jesus

Primary path. If you are reading the library in order, begin here before moving to the other teachers.

  • The Living Way (Read · PDF)
  • The Living Suttas (Read · PDF)
  • Wisdom Gospels (Philip, Mary, Thomas — closest to original words)
  • A Guide to the Way of the Living Jesus
  • The Syncretic Christ (essay)

These scrolls lean toward the early “religion of Yeshua”—inner transformation and direct knowing—more than later doctrine. They are not written to attack church Christianity, but to uncover the deeper stream beneath it.
